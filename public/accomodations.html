Accomodations

Our Nicaragua hotel accommodations are the most lovely and spacious of any San Juan Del Sur resort. Pelican Eyes lodging guestrooms feature soaring cane ceilings, graceful arched windows, airy terraces, exposed natural woods, handcrafted furniture and Central American artwork. It is the ideal location for families, couples and travelers of all types.

All accommodations generally feature: Air conditioning and flow-through natural ventilation with ceiling fans, Satellite TV and stereo, and DVD, Convenience Kitchen (including a small microwave, mini-refrigerator, and coffee maker) OR Gourmet Kitchen (including a large Microwave, full gas stove and oven, large refrigerator with ice maker, coffee maker, fully equipped with a wide array of cooking utensils, pots, and pans, and table settings for 8, Private patio or terrace, Large private bathroom with hot water.

Tips for Travelers & Guests:

Do not bring Travelers Checks.They simply are not the time honored currency exchange system for travelers that they once were. Nobody accepts them in Nicaragua!
Best to bring only Credit Cardsand Cash Cards that can be used in Cash Machines (remember to have authorized PIN Codes as you will not be able to obtain them here).
Also, report to your Credit Card companies that you will be traveling in Nicaragua (and wherever else you will be going) to prevent having to have your identify checked after your first three purchases or credit card uses.
US dollars are accepted throughout Nicaragua.Bring a mixture of large and small bills. Most businesses will NOT change nor except $100 bills. No one will accept bills with damage, including the tiniest mark or tear.
